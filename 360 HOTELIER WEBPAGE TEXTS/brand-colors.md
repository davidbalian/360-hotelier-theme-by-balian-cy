**360 Hotelier Brand Colour Palette**

**Primary Brand Colours**

**🟣 Strategic Purple (Primary Brand Colour)**

Used for headlines, key highlights, icons.

Meaning:

- Strategy
- Expertise
- Premium hospitality consulting

**🟠 Mediterranean Gold / Orange (Energy Colour)**

Used for buttons, calls-to-action, highlights.

HEX: **#F28C28**

Meaning:

- Cyprus sunshine
- Growth and energy
- Tourism and optimism connected to Cyprus

**Supporting Colours**

**⚫ Deep Charcoal (Professional Text Colour)**

HEX: **#2B2B2B**

Use for:

- Website body text
- Documents
- Presentations

Why:

- Softer and more elegant than pure black.

**🏖 Mediterranean Sand (Warm Background)**

HEX: **#F2E6D9**

Use for:

- Section backgrounds
- Cards
- Website blocks

Why:

- Creates warmth and elegance inspired by Cyprus beaches and stone architecture.

**⚪ Soft White (Clean Space)**

HEX: **#FAFAFA**

Use for:

- Main website background
- Clean modern look
- White space balance

**Optional Accent Colour**

**🌊 Mediterranean Blue (Tourism Accent)**

HEX: **#2C6E91**

Use sparingly for:

- Links
- Hover effects

- The Mediterranean Sea

**Visual Balance for Website**

Ideal usage ratio:

| **Colour**   | **Usage** |
| ------------ | --------- |
| White / Sand | 60%       |
| Purple       | 20%       |
| Charcoal     | 10%       |
| Orange       | 8%        |
| Blue accent  | 2%        |

This creates a **clean consulting look** while keeping the brand **warm and Mediterranean**.
