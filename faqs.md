What is the difference between hotel consulting and revenue management?

Revenue management focuses mainly on pricing and demand strategy.

Hotel consulting goes further and includes:

• sales strategy
• OTA management
• market positioning
• distribution planning
• commercial strategy
• performance improvement
In short, revenue management is one part of hotel consulting, while consulting covers the full growth strategy.

How often should hotel prices be updated?

Hotel prices should be reviewed at least once per week and more frequently during high season.

Pricing usually changes based on:

• demand
• competitor pricing
• booking pace
• events
• seasonality
• market trends
Active pricing management helps hotels maximize both occupancy and revenue.
